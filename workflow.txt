User uploads audio (Flask UI)
        ↓
Flask saves file locally
        ↓
FFmpeg converts audio → mono WAV
        ↓
Whisper transcribes audio → segments + text
        ↓
Segment-wise speaker embeddings extracted
        ↓
Clustering groups segments by speaker
        ↓
Speaker labels assigned (Speaker 1, 2…)
        ↓
Final transcript generated
        ↓
Groq LLM analyzes sentiment
        ↓
JSON response returned to frontend
